Rules

Format of the Tournament

This will be a Round Robin tournament and Matches will be held every Saturday and Sunday

NCL Management Team will provide a time slot during which the match will be conducted
Player Criteria for Teams

Match will be 14 vs 14 players out of which at least 10 should be present on the server to start the match and in any case match will not be delayed for more than 10 minutes

If less than 10 players are available, the other team wins
Maps for Tournament

Maps will be decided by a knife round between captain (or any other player nominated by the captain) of that team

Map Pool:

  • de_dust2
  • de_inferno
  • de_train
  • de_nuke
  • de_mirage
Matches will be BO3
No repetition of maps in the same match
Each map will be chosen by 1v1 knife
Choosing Sides in Match

CT or T will be decided by a team knife round, players should not camp and try to end it asap

If less than 10 players are available, the other team wins
Match Results

First team to win 16 rounds will win that map, OverTime will be conducted in case of a tie, swap after 3 rounds and the first team to win 4 rounds wins the match

Infinite OT until either team wins
Timeouts in Match

Each team gets 3 timeouts for each map, in case of Overtime timeouts will reset to 1 for both teams

Remaining timeouts if any will be removed in OT
DDoS in Match

In case of DDoS or lag the match will be paused and will be resumed from the same score going on

In case of server crash we will still resume from the same score
Recording Demos

Demo will be auto recorded, and no one should stop recording demo

Players playing from cafe's should take a backup of their demo as soon as the match ends, you may take pen drives along with you and can upload demos from your home later
The player who is unable to provide match demo will be disqualified from the tournament
Request for Demos

Captains can ask up to 3 demos of their opposite team players, in case you want demo of certain players contact NCL Management Team

Players playing from cafe's should take a backup of their demo as soon as the match ends, you may take pen drives along with you and can upload demos from your home later
If any player is not able to provide his demo after match after being asked by the opposite teamâ€™s captains or NCL Management Team, then that player will be banned and player's team will will lose that match
Punishment for Hacking

If any player is found hacking during a match, then that player will be banned and player's team will will lose that match

No Rematch at any Condition
Auto-Bans by Server

If any player gets banned for any script/hacks or for AWP crosshair by server plugin then he will be disqualified from NCL

Scroll ducking is not allowed
Sharing Steam IDs

Any player found playing from two IDs or someone else playing from registered players ID, the ID will be banned from tournament along with the player he played for

We have plugins to track IP addresses
Dispute Resolution

In case of any kind of dispute in match or regarding any issue faced in between the match should be conveyed by the captain directly to NCL Management Team or in captains group

Posts on main group will be ignored

In case of any kind of dispute the decision of NCL Management Team will be FINAL
